MEng (Hons) Mechatronics Engineering

September 2021 - July 2025

Ever since I was a kid, I've been fascinated by how things work, from fast cars to 'simple' things like a vacuum cleaner which is how I ended up in Mechatronic Engineering. Little did I know that meant signing up for endless brain torture, sleepless nights, and the occasional existential crisis... but honestly, I wouldn't have it any other way. After 4 years of blood, sweat, and not touching grass, I graduated with First Class Honours in Mechatronic Engineering from the University of Nottingham Malaysia.
